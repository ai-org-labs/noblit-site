Noblit

A local-first writing app for turning Markdown-like text into documents, slides, tables, Mermaid diagrams, and ArchMap diagrams on macOS.

Download for macOS Support

Noblit is distributed outside the Mac App Store as a Developer ID signed macOS app.

Readable from text

Paste or import local text, keep editing on the left, and preview the result as a clean document or slide deck.

  • Local workspace under ~/Noblit
  • Autosaved drafts for recovery
  • Export to Markdown, HTML, and PDF

Local-first

Your documents stay on your Mac. Noblit does not require an account or cloud workspace for the first release.

Preview-driven

Render Markdown-style notes as documents, slide decks, tables, Mermaid diagrams, and ArchMap diagrams while you edit.

Share when ready

Export explicit artifacts when you choose. Noblit keeps editing, autosave, and export as separate actions.
