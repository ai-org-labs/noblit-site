Your work saves itself

No Save button. Edits stream straight to a local file as you type, with a quiet “Saved ✓”. Crash-recovery drafts are there if you ever need them.

Document & Slide, one source

View the same text as a readable document or a slide deck. Hit Present to send slides to an external window for a second screen.

Diagrams & tables that open up

Mermaid, ArchMap, tables, and task lists render as you write, with focused viewers for pan, zoom, and editing right where the block lives.

Local-first and private

Everything lives in a workspace on your machine. No account, no cloud, no telemetry. Import copies files in; export is always your explicit choice.

Paste to polish, in three steps

The whole loop stays on your desktop.

1

Paste or import

Drop in AI output, Markdown, notes, or a file. Noblit copies it into your local library as an editable source.

2

See it read well

The live preview turns your source into a clean document or slides, with diagrams and tables rendered inline.

3

Present or export

Present to an external window, or export to Markdown, HTML, or PDF when you decide the piece is ready.
